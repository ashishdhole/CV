Ashish Dhole

I am a Ph.D. research scholar in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at IIT Bombay, India. I am currently associated with The National Facility of Orientation Imaging Microscopy (OIM) and Texture.

Research Interests: High Temperature Materials, Computational Material Science - Density Functional Theory (DFT), Microstructural Engineering, Thermomechanical Processing, Materials Characterization and Oxidation
